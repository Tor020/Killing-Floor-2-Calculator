Note: All damage numbers for melee weapons are taken at their highest values:

Damage dealt by melee weapons depend on attack direction and how well melee tracer was connected with ZEDs body - vertical strikes do more damage while horizontal strikes do less damage but have better AOE. Pure left or right (non-diagonal) strikes do never more than 75% and never less than 50% of the base weapon damage at a distance of player's viewport. Stab attacks are always doing full damage due to tracer nature.
